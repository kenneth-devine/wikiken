Vampire X Vampire Hunter Romance

This story was inspired by my days of binge reading romance manga. The story follows a young man in the profession of vampire hunter. It is set in the modern world, where the supernatural is not public knowledge. The ancient vampiric families and the organizations hunting them are in constant conflict. The main character is sent to a town after unusual occurences occur nearby. He attends the school there, and meets a girl who he suspects to be a vampire. He gets close to her, trying to learn about any other vampires. Once her true identity is revealed, he's left with a difficult choice: slay her now, or fully commit to extracting information... by wooing her. He begins to go out with her, and she begins to reveal details about her vampiric family. As his feeling grow for her, he's trapped between his duty, desire, and the pain of lying to the one you love. She on the other hand, is also hiding their relationship from her family, while holding her bloodlust back. Could be fun.
